# Payment Methods

Alpine Forge accepts the following payment methods at checkout:

- **Credit and debit cards**: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- **Apple Pay** and **Google Pay**
- **Shop Pay** (for returning customers)
- **Affirm**: buy now, pay over 3, 6, or 12 months for orders over $100
- **Gift cards** purchased from alpineforge.example

We do not accept checks, wire transfers, cryptocurrency, PayPal, or cash-on-delivery.

## Charges

Your card is authorized at order placement and charged when the order ships. For pre-orders, we authorize at order placement and charge 24 hours before ship date.

## Currency

Prices are listed in USD. International customers are charged in USD; your bank determines the exchange rate and may add a foreign-transaction fee.

## Sales tax

We collect sales tax in states where we have nexus, based on the ship-to address. Oregon, Delaware, Montana, New Hampshire, and Alaska do not collect state sales tax. International orders do not include US sales tax, but import duties and VAT may apply in the destination country.

## Security

All payment data is handled by Stripe and never touches Alpine Forge servers. Our checkout is PCI-DSS Level 1 compliant.
