# About Alpine Forge

Alpine Forge is a small outdoor gear company based in Bend, Oregon. We design and test our own products, with final assembly split between Portland (soft goods) and contract manufacturers in Vietnam and Taiwan (hardgoods and hardware).

## Our story

We started in 2017 as a two-person shop making ultralight backpacks for the Pacific Crest Trail. The Ridge Runner 35L Backpack was our first product and is still in the line today, now in its fourth generation.

## Design philosophy

Every product has a single lead designer who is responsible for it from sketch to shipment. We prototype in our Bend workshop and test with a rotating panel of guides, athletes, and regular customers. If a feature doesn't earn its weight, we cut it.

## Sustainability

- All DWR treatments have been PFC-free since 2024.
- Down is certified to the Responsible Down Standard.
- We ship orders in recycled-content poly mailers; no plastic bags inside for soft goods.
- Our Bend office runs on 100% renewable electricity through Pacific Power's Blue Sky program.
- We offer at-cost repairs on most apparel and packs to keep gear out of landfills.
