# Account Help

## Creating an account

You can check out as a guest, but creating an account lets you track orders, save shipping addresses, and start returns or warranty claims without looking up the order number.

Sign up at alpineforge.example/register using your email. You'll receive a verification link — click it to activate the account.

## Forgot your password?

Go to alpineforge.example/forgot-password and enter the email on file. We email a reset link that expires in 30 minutes. If the email doesn't arrive within 10 minutes, check your spam folder, and make sure noreply@alpineforge.example is on your allowlist.

## Changing your email

Log in, go to Account → Profile, and click "Change email." We send a confirmation link to the new address to verify it before the change takes effect.

## Closing your account

Email privacy@alpineforge.example from the address on file. We'll delete your personal information and order history within 30 days. Pending returns or warranty claims must be resolved first.

## Order history

You can see every order placed on your account under Account → Orders. Orders placed as a guest are not attached to the account; use the order number + email lookup at alpineforge.example/orders to find those.

## Loyalty program

Alpine Forge doesn't currently run a loyalty or points program. We occasionally email exclusive offers to email-list subscribers.
