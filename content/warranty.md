# Warranty

Alpine Forge stands behind our gear with a limited lifetime warranty against defects in materials and workmanship. If your gear fails due to a manufacturing defect, we'll repair it, replace it, or refund it — whichever makes the most sense.

## What's covered

- Stitching and seam failures
- Zipper and buckle failures
- Delamination of waterproof membranes (Summit Shell Jacket, Torrent Rain Pants)
- Down leakage beyond normal shedding (Ember Down Hoodie 800FP, Cascade 20°F Sleeping Bag)
- Pole breakage under normal use (Cirrus Ultralight Tent 2P, Basecamp Dome Tent 4P, Quickdraw Trekking Poles)
- Sole delamination on footwear within two years of purchase (Pinnacle Trail Runner)

## What's not covered

- Normal wear and tear (abrasion, fading, odor)
- Damage from misuse, accidents, or improper care
- Animal damage
- Lost or stolen items
- Alterations by a third party

## How to file a claim

Email warranty@alpineforge.example with:
1. Your order number or proof of purchase
2. Photos of the issue
3. A short description of how the gear failed

We typically respond within 3 business days. If the claim is approved, we'll send a prepaid shipping label. Repairs take 2–4 weeks from when we receive the gear; replacements ship immediately if in stock.
