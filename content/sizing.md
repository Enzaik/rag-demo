# Sizing Guide

Alpine Forge apparel runs true to size for most customers. When in doubt, size up for layering. All measurements are body measurements, not garment.

## Tops (unisex)

Applies to Summit Shell Jacket, Ember Down Hoodie 800FP, Glacier Merino Base Layer, Zephyr Cycling Windbreaker.

| Size | Chest (in) | Chest (cm) |
|------|-----------:|-----------:|
| XS   | 33–35      | 84–89      |
| S    | 36–38      | 91–97      |
| M    | 39–41      | 99–104     |
| L    | 42–44      | 107–112    |
| XL   | 45–47      | 114–119    |

## Bottoms (unisex, waist size)

Applies to Trailblazer Hiking Pants, Torrent Rain Pants.

Waist size is labeled in inches. Trailblazer offers three inseam lengths: 30" (76cm), 32" (81cm), and 34" (86cm).

## Footwear

Pinnacle Trail Runner is available in US men's sizing 7–13 (whole sizes only). Women should size down 1.5 from their normal women's US size. Customers with wider feet typically size up by a half.

## Gloves

Tundra Insulated Mittens sizing is based on hand circumference (across knuckles, not including thumb):

| Size | Circumference (in) |
|------|-------------------:|
| S    | 7.0–7.5            |
| M    | 7.5–8.0            |
| L    | 8.0–9.0            |
| XL   | 9.0–10.0           |

## Fit notes

- The Summit Shell Jacket is cut roomy for a mid-layer (we recommend pairing with the Ember Down Hoodie 800FP).
- Trailblazer Hiking Pants have an athletic cut through the thigh.
- Zephyr Cycling Windbreaker is cut slim to avoid flapping at speed.
