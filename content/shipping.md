# Shipping

Alpine Forge ships orders from our warehouse in Bend, Oregon within one business day of order placement. Orders placed after 1pm Pacific ship the next business day.

## Domestic (United States)

- **Standard** (3–7 business days): free on orders over $75, otherwise $6.95 flat.
- **Expedited** (2 business days): $14.95 flat.
- **Overnight**: $34.95 flat. Order by 11am Pacific for same-day dispatch.

We ship to all 50 states, APO/FPO addresses, and US territories.

## International

We ship to Canada, Mexico, the UK, the EU, Australia, New Zealand, and Japan. Rates are calculated at checkout based on weight and destination. Most international orders arrive within 7–14 business days.

The customer is responsible for any import duties or taxes charged by the destination country. Alpine Forge is not able to pre-pay international duties.

## Large and oversized items

Tents (Cirrus Ultralight Tent 2P, Basecamp Dome Tent 4P) and cast iron cookware (Campfire Cast Iron Skillet 10in) ship via ground only and are not eligible for overnight service. International shipping for oversized items is quoted separately — contact us for a quote before placing your order.

## Tracking

Every shipment includes a tracking number emailed once the carrier scans the package. If you haven't seen a tracking email within 48 hours of placing your order, check your spam folder and then reach out to support@alpineforge.example.
