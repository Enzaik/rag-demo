# Frequently Asked Questions

## Are Alpine Forge down products ethically sourced?

Yes. All down in our products (Ember Down Hoodie 800FP, Cascade 20°F Sleeping Bag) is certified to the Responsible Down Standard. We audit our supplier annually.

## Is the shell fabric on the Summit Shell Jacket PFC-free?

Yes. Since 2024 all Alpine Forge DWR treatments are C0 (PFC-free). This covers the Summit Shell Jacket, Torrent Rain Pants, and Zephyr Cycling Windbreaker.

## Do the trekking poles work with smart phones for photos?

Quickdraw Trekking Poles do not have a camera mount threading. We do not currently sell pole-to-camera adapters.

## Is the Cascade 20°F Sleeping Bag rated comfort or lower-limit?

The 20°F number is the EN/ISO comfort rating. The lower-limit rating is approximately 8°F.

## Can I use the Campfire Cast Iron Skillet on a glass cooktop?

Yes, but slide — don't set down sharply — to avoid cracking the glass. The skillet weighs 2.1 kg.

## What's the actual weight of the Cirrus Ultralight Tent 2P?

1180 grams for the tent, poles, stakes, and stuff sacks combined. Our "minimum trail weight" is 1020 grams (without stuff sacks and extra stakes).

## Does the Helios Solar Lantern's solar panel charge quickly enough to rely on?

No. The solar panel is intended as a top-up in the field, not a primary charging method. Expect 8–10 hours of direct sunlight for a full charge from empty. Use the USB-C port for a 3-hour full charge before a trip.

## Do the Pinnacle Trail Runners come in wide?

Not currently. We recommend sizing up a half if you have a wider foot.

## Are the Vista 8x42 Binoculars tripod-mountable?

Yes, there's a standard 1/4"-20 threaded mount under the center hinge cap.
